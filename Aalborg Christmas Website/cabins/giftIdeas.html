Gift Ideas

Krystalhulen

Krystalhulen can be found on Gammeltorv - this is the first time they are at the Christmas market. They have a large selection of crystals and crystal lamps. Krystalhulen has a store in Turøgade 10, 9000 Aalborg.

Kunsthåndværk

Their crafts can be found at Gammeltorv. The cottage is run by seven independent artists. Here, quality Christmas decorations and gifts are sold in all price ranges. What you can be absolutely sure of is that everything is "Made in Denmark". Here you will find ceramics, jewelery, glass and wicker.

Tupperware

You can find Tupperware at Gammeltorv. In their cottage you will find plastic things for every household. They are best known for their popular ecopure bottles. By choosing eco + Bottles, you save the planet 14 kg of disposable plastic per. year. Have a talk with their skilled consultants - they can definitely help you.
