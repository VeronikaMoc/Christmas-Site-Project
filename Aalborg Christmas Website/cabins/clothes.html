Jule-Sweaters ApS

Jule-Sweaters ApS is one of Europe's largest Christmas sweater brands. The company is started and run by two northerners. In 5 years, the company has gone from producing 1,000 sweaters to producing more than 300,000 Christmas sweaters in 2021. In addition, the company has a GOTS Certificate, which is one of the highest textile remarks within Sustainability. They have hand-picked some of their favorite designs, which you can find in their cabin on Nytorv - stop by and find your favorite.
