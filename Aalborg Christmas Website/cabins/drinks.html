Ma Petite France

You can find the French wine bar Ma Petite France on Nytorv - this is the first time they are at the Christmas market. They sell red and white vin chaud - the mulled wine of the French - for this you of course get a cookie. In addition, they also sell unique handmade Christmas decorations from Miss Green Design. Ma Petite France has a French wine bar in Nørregade 6, 9000 Aalborg.

ROMeriet

You can find the ROMeriet at Gammeltorv. Here they sellhot cocoa with rum, freshly whipped whipped cream with vanilla sugar on top and chocolate sprinkle. Children hot cocoa - freshly whipped whipped cream with vanilla sugar on top and chocolate sprinkle. Romtoddy: Christmas tea with cinnamon sticks, lemon, cane sugar and cloves, which is boiled up and added to rum before serving and of course homemade from scratch. They also sell their rum and Christmas rum. ROMeriet has a store in Urbansgade 22A, 9000 Aalborg.

Gløggkompagniet & Skotlander

Gløggkompagniet is new to the Christmas market and known from Tivoli in Copenhagen. This year you will find them at our Christmas market in two cabins on Gammeltorv. Here you can buy delicious mulled wine, hot chocolate and Danish lumumba with homemade rum from northern Jutland. In addition, they have warm apple slices, caffe latte, warm elderflower, chocolate and delicious Christmas drinks / cocktails.

På Fjorden

På Fjorden is participating in the Christmas market for the first time. You will find their cabin on Nytorv. They will be selling "winter soup of the day", their own delicious apple mulled wine and a selection of their fantastic Christmas cakes which vary from day to day. In addition, they have coffee, a selection of hot chocolate and hot spicy Christmas tea. Restaurant is located on Østerågade 27 on the 1st floor of BoShop.

Hos Linda

Linda's waffles and Linda's sweets are Linda's cabins at the Christmas market and you will find them on Gammeltorv. Linda sells a large selection of old-fashioned sweets. In addition, you can also buy sweets, delicious waffles with jam, roasted almonds and lollipops from Sugarpilots. Sugarpilots are handmade lollipops with different motifs. They are made from organic sugar and 100% natural vegan ingredients.At Linda you will also find a large selection of drinks. Tea, coffee, cocoa, juice from Søbogaard, soda and water

Caféen

The Caféen is again this year located on Gammeltorv next to the Ferris wheel. Warm apple slices, coffee, cocoa, mulled wine, mulled wine, beer, irish coffee, lumumba etc. are sold here.
