Food

The Fudge Factory

The Fudge Factory can be found on Nytorv. They sell handmade English fudge, truffles and nougat. Joey, who owns the stall, imports it himself from England and it is his family that produces it. At the Christmas market, they will have about 65 different flavors and they have added vegan Fudge to their selection as well.

På Fjorden

På Fjorden is participating in the Christmas market for the first time. You will find their cabin on Nytorv. They will be selling "winter soup of the day", their own delicious apple mulled wine and a selection of their fantastic Christmas cakes which vary from day to day. In addition, they have coffee, a selection of hot chocolate and hot spicy Christmas tea. Restaurant is located on Østerågade 27 on the 1st floor of BoShop.

Grill Hytten

Grill Hytten you will find again this year on Gammeltorv. Here they serve Christmas frankfurter, HUGE hot dogs and 1/2 meter long hot dogs with raw pickled red onions - a real Christmas classic. All their sausages are grilled directly over an open fire on the large octagonal grill. In addition, they also sell cold and hot drinks and other delicacies.

Hos Linda

Linda's waffles and Linda's sweets are Linda's cabins at the Christmas market and you will find them on Gammeltorv. Linda sells a large selection of old-fashioned sweets. In addition, you can also buy sweets, delicious waffles with jam, roasted almonds and lollipops from Sugarpilots. Sugarpilots are handmade lollipops with different motifs. They are made from organic sugar and 100% natural vegan ingredients.At Linda you will also find a large selection of drinks. Tea, coffee, cocoa, juice from Søbogaard, soda and water

Hollandsk Ost

You will find Hollandsk Ost on Gammeltorv. This is the eighth time they are at the Christmas market. They sell a large selection of quality Dutch cheese, Dutch sausages and Dutch stroopwafels (honey waffles).

Kagespecialisten

The cake specialist is new to the Christmas market. You will find their cabin on Gammeltorv. They sell different types of Romanian cakes and homemade pralines with Christmas fillings such as orange, cinnamon, etc., as well as hot waffles with chocolate.

Brændte mandler

You will find the cabin with roasted almonds on Gammeltorv. As the name suggests, they sell roasted almonds. In addition, they also sell popcorn, other snacks, a large selection of lollipops, candyfloss and candied apples.
