Ma Petite France

You can find the French wine bar Ma Petite France on Nytorv - this is the first time they are at the Christmas market. They sell red and white vin chaud - the mulled wine of the French - for this you of course get a cookie. In addition, they also sell unique handmade Christmas decorations from Miss Green Design. Ma Petite France has a French wine bar in Nørregade 6, 9000 Aalborg.

Studenterbiksens Juletræssalg

You can find Studenterbiksen's Christmas tree sale at Gammeltorv. They sell various Christmas trees. Among other things, potted trees, small ornamental trees and cut trees. In addition, you can also buy Advent wreaths and other Christmas decorations.

Kunsthåndværk

Their crafts can be found at Gammeltorv. The cottage is run by seven independent artists. Here, quality Christmas decorations and gifts are sold in all price ranges. What you can be absolutely sure of is that everything is "Made in Denmark". Here you will find ceramics, jewelery, glass and wicker.

Aalborg Zoo

Aalborg Zoo can be found this time on Nytorv. The theme for their cabin is Greenland. They will sell polar bear teddy bears, leather gloves, skins and other things that fit into the Greenlandic theme. In addition, they will also be selling items from Great Greenland.
