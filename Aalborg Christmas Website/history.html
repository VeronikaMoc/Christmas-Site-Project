Get to know more about Aalborg Christmas Market

lightning of the Christmas tree

Located at GammelTorv Aalborg Christmas market was founded in 2002. Throughout the years it’s been one of the biggest Christmas markets in whole northland, usually containing approximately 20-30 Christmas booths. They sell everything starting from hot wine, rum with tea, roasted almonds to cloths and Christmas decorations. They always have Christmas tree light-up at 18. november, and it’s always Santa Claus who arrives with his boat to light it up.

Where does the tree comes from ?

The tree is a gift from Aalborg sisters town in Norway and Aalborg commune is the one who is providing the help to move it to Aalborg every year. This year is also quite special due the fact that the Aalborg Christmas market is now located no only on GammelTorv street, but also on Nytorv Street, so come along and see theynew Aalborg Christmas market from 15.november till 23.december.
